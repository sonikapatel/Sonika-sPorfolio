      Shotgun

      Mobile Application
      Shotgun

      Shotgun is a mobile application that organizes interstate and intrastate rides for college students. Currently, universities have rideshare groups online. Students have to search endlessly through the facebook page to find an accommodation that best suits their carpooling needs. With Shotgun, students can filter their preferences, including preferred date, time, and destination locations, to schedule cheap long-distance rides, whether it is to visit their home for the weekend or attend a concert, too far and too expensive for an Uber. Before creating these high-fidelty wireframes, I created personas and scenarios. To view the prototype of the application, click here.
